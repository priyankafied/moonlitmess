Moonlit Mess

it's quiet here

you can leave what you're carrying

stays in your browser only. never stored.

that's okay.
you don't have to release all of it now.
come back when you're ready.

someone left this for you

leave a small, human thought
for a stranger to find tonight.

this is the only part that is shared — anonymously.

someone will find this.
thank you for leaving something soft behind.
